CHANGING THEMES
---------------

1. Log in to your portfolio
2. Go to Dashboard
3. Click "Edit" button
4. Scroll to "Theme & Settings" section
5. Click on any theme to apply it
6. Click "Save Changes"
7. View your portfolio to see the new theme

All themes are fully responsive and work on mobile, tablet, and desktop.

MULTI-LANGUAGE SUPPORT
----------------------

All themes automatically adapt to your resume's language:
- German resume → German labels
- French resume → French labels  
- English resume → English labels
- Korean, Spanish, etc. all supported

The theme styling remains the same, only the UI labels change.
